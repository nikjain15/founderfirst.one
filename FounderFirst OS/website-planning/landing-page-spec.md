## Tone sanity check

Read every line aloud:
> *Would a caring, knowledgeable human bookkeeper say this to a busy business owner?*

**Watch-outs**
- No "operating layer", "infrastructure", "empowering", "unlock", "boost"
- No "ambitious founders"
- No more than 2 short sentences per paragraph
- One idea per line
- Emojis: only 🎉 👋 ✓ 💪 — sparingly
