Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This policy explains what information FounderFirst collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It is written to be honest and specific rather than exhaustive. If anything here is unclear, email us at nik@founderfirst.one.

Who we are

FounderFirst builds operating software for business owners, including Penny, an assistant you can reach by web chat and through Discord direct messages.

What we collect

  • Account & waitlist details — the email address and any information you provide when joining the waitlist or signing up.
  • Usage analytics — basic, privacy-conscious product analytics about how our marketing pages are used, subject to your cookie consent choices.
  • Penny conversation content — the messages you exchange with Penny, including over Discord (covered in detail below).

Penny conversations on Discord

When you talk to Penny through Discord direct messages, we store the conversation so Penny can remember context and so we have a support and audit record. Please read this section carefully.

What is stored

  • Every message — both your messages and Penny's replies are stored, associated with your Discord user ID, in our backend database (hosted with our infrastructure provider, Supabase).
  • A rolling summary — to keep conversations coherent over time, we also keep a periodically updated summary of your earlier messages.

Disconnecting does not delete your history

If you run /disconnect, or if an administrator revokes your Discord connection, Penny starts fresh and stops using your past conversation as live context. This does not erase your data. Your prior messages are archived — retained in our backend as a support and record-keeping record — and the working summary is cleared. The same applies when older messages are folded into a summary during normal use: they are archived, not deleted.

Why we keep it

We retain Discord conversation transcripts to provide and improve support, to investigate issues and disputes, and to maintain an accurate record of interactions with Penny. We keep this data for as long as we reasonably need it for those purposes, unless you ask us to delete it.

How to delete your data

You can request permanent deletion of your Penny conversation data at any time — this is a genuine, irreversible erasure that removes both your stored messages and your summary, not merely an archive. To make a request, email nik@founderfirst.one from the email associated with your account, or include your Discord username, and ask us to delete your Penny conversation data. We will honor verified deletion requests and carry out the full erasure.

How we use information

  • To operate Penny and provide the features you ask for.
  • To provide customer support and resolve issues.
  • To keep records of interactions for audit and dispute purposes.
  • To communicate with you about access, updates, and your account.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with infrastructure and service providers (such as our database host and analytics provider) only to the extent needed to operate the product, and with authorities where required by law.

Security

Conversation data is stored in a database with access restricted to our backend services. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or deletion requests: nik@founderfirst.one.
