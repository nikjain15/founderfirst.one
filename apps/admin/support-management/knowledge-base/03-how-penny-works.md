# How Penny works

Penny is a conversation, not a chore. Here's the whole interaction.

## Setup

Connect Stripe, your bank, your card — anywhere money moves. That's the setup. No spreadsheets to import, no chart-of-accounts to configure.

## Day to day

Penny watches your accounts 24/7 and sorts every transaction the way your CPA needs.

A few times a week she'll ping you with a question — usually "business or personal?" One tap answers it. Done.

She remembers your answers, so next time a similar transaction shows up she handles it on her own.

## What you see

- **Real profit**, updated as the money moves. Not just revenue — what you're actually making after costs.
- **Tax readiness** — a live view of how close your books are to being filing-ready.
- **Late invoices** — Penny shows you what's overdue and nudges the client for you.

## How invoice nudging works

When a client is late paying, Penny sends a professional reminder from your email address, written in your voice. The client doesn't know it's automated. You don't have to write the awkward "just checking in" email yourself.
