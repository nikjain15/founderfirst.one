# Pricing and early access

## Where Penny is right now

Penny is in early access. We're opening seats in small batches as we make sure each new founder gets a clean experience.

## The waitlist offer

Join the waitlist and you get **3 months of Penny on us** when your seat opens. No card needed to claim the spot — you only enter payment details when you start.

## Referral months

Every founder you refer to the waitlist adds **one extra free month** to your account, up to **12 months total**. Refer 9 founders, get a full year free on top of the 3 months.

## After the free months

Pricing is still being finalized as we learn from early-access founders. We'll communicate it clearly before your free period ends — never a surprise charge. If the pricing doesn't work for you, you cancel and keep whatever clean books Penny has already produced.

Drop your email at [founderfirst.one](https://founderfirst.one). That's the whole sign-up — 10 seconds.
