# Penny — your autonomous 24/7 bookkeeper

Penny is FounderFirst's first product. She's an autonomous bookkeeper who watches your accounts around the clock and keeps your books clean without you thinking about it.

## What Penny does

- **Watches every account where money moves.** Stripe, your bank, your credit card, PayPal — anywhere transactions happen.
- **Categorizes every transaction** the way a CPA needs it. Income, expenses, the right buckets for taxes.
- **Chases late invoices for you.** Professional reminders, sent from your email, in your voice. You stop nagging clients.
- **Keeps books tax-ready year-round.** No scramble in April. CPA-export ready every day of the year.

## What Penny gives you

- **Know what you're actually making.** Real profit, updated as the money moves — not just revenue.
- **Never chase a late payment again.** Penny nudges clients so you don't have to.
- **No scramble at tax time.** Books stay clean and CPA-ready.

## For business owners

No spreadsheets. No chasing receipts. Just clean books.

## For CPAs

Penny does the data entry your client always forgets and queues it for you. You review, approve, and export in your usual format. Keep QuickBooks or Xero — nothing to migrate. Every transaction categorized, every receipt attached.
