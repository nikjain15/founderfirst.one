Privacy Policy — Signals Capture extension

Last updated: June 21, 2026

In short: This is an internal tool for the FounderFirst team. It only does anything when a team member explicitly clicks “Capture” on a post they’ve selected, and it sends that post solely to FounderFirst’s own backend. It does not track browsing, run in the background, use analytics, or sell or share data with anyone.

What the extension is

“FounderFirst Signals — Capture” is a Chrome extension used by the FounderFirst team to capture a public social or community post (for example on Reddit, LinkedIn, Facebook, or X) into FounderFirst’s internal admin, where it is reviewed as a potential sales lead.

What data it accesses, and when

  • Only on an explicit action. When a team member selects text on a page and clicks the “Capture to FounderFirst Signals” menu item, the extension reads the selected post’s text, author name, and link from that page at that moment.
  • Local settings. The extension stores its configured endpoint URL and a shared access secret in your browser’s local storage so it knows where to send captured posts. These are not personal data and are never shared.
  • It does not read pages in the background, track your browsing history, record keystrokes, or collect personal, financial, health, location, or authentication information.

How data is used and shared

  • A captured post (its text, author, and link) is sent to FounderFirst’s own backend (hosted on Supabase) for the sole purpose of reviewing it as a potential lead.
  • We do not sell or transfer this data to third parties.
  • We do not use the data for any purpose unrelated to the extension’s single purpose above, nor for creditworthiness or lending.

Data retention & deletion

Captured posts are stored in FounderFirst’s admin database and can be deleted by the team at any time. To request deletion of captured data, contact us at the address below.
