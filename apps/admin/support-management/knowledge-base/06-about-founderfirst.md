# About FounderFirst

## The mission

Give business owners the back-office support that used to only exist inside bigger companies. The kind of support a founder of a 50-person business takes for granted — clean books, someone watching cash flow, someone chasing late invoices — delivered to a solo founder for almost nothing.

## Who's behind it

FounderFirst is built by Nik Jain — three-time founder, Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia. GitHub: [nikjain15](https://github.com/nikjain15).

## How we work

We ship in small batches. Penny went out as an early-access product to a small group of founders before opening more broadly, and the same approach applies to every product after her. We'd rather a few hundred founders love it than a few thousand tolerate it.

## What's next after Penny

The roadmap is driven by what early-access founders ask for. Bookkeeping is the loudest pain we found in our research, so it's where we're starting — but the long-term picture is an operating system for the whole back office. We'll communicate what's next as it gets close to shipping, not before.
